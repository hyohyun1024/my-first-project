📚 어휘 (Vocabulary)

비슷해 보이지만 뉘앙스가 다른 단어들 — 구분해서 익혀두세요!

UPGRADE Week 8

further vs additional

△ needs additional review (단순 추가)
✓ needs further review
further = 더 나아가는 추가 (확장의 뉘앙스)
additional = 단순히 하나 더 추가
UPGRADE Week 10

simply vs just

△ just looking for VC Network (부정적 뉘앙스)
✓ simply looking for VC Network
중립적 "단순히"를 표현할 때 → simply / solely
KEY PHRASE Week 8

validated to a certain level

validated to a certain level
"특정 수준까지 검증된"

startups that are validated to a certain level
PAIR Week 10

persuade / convince

설득하다
persuade = 행동을 하도록 설득
convince = 납득·믿게끔 설득

I had to persuade/convince him to agree
PAIR Week 10

diverse / varying

상이한, 다양한
diverse = 다양한 (종류·유형이 다름)
varying = 상이한, 제각각인

align our diverse / varying needs
IDIOM Week 10

a couple of = 2개 정도

✓ He has a couple of weeks off
구어체에서 숫자 2는 "two" 대신 "a couple of"를 씁니다.
KEY WORD Week 8

getting better / recovering

나아지다, 회복하다
✓ He's getting better / recovering
"조금 나아보여" → He looks a bit better
"점점 낫고 있어" → He's getting better and better
UPGRADE Week 10

to set a goal for

"내년도 목표 설정을 위해"
✓ to set a goal for next year
PHRASE Week 10

"which isn't easy" 구조

"이건 쉽지 않아"를 자연스럽게
△ ...and it is not easy to do
✓ ..., which isn't easy
KEY WORD Week 10

expand into (확장하다)

"노출"로 직역하지 말고 동사 expand를 사용하세요.

to expand into overseas markets
(해외 시장에 진출·확장하기 위해)
PAIR Week 8

going well vs doing well

going well = 업무·프로젝트 진행 상황
doing well = 사람의 건강·컨디션

IC preparation is going well
PAIR Week 10

He has a job vs He's working

He has a job = 직업이 있다 (상태)
He's working = 지금 일하고 있다 (행위)

일상 맥락에서는 "He's working"이 더 자연스럽습니다.

Key Collocations

💼 비즈니스 영어

d.camp 업무 맥락에서 실제로 쓰이는 표현들 — Cheatsheet 기반

Q1
Who are your shareholders?
All major Korean banks made donations to d.camp (in 2013 and 2018) — they're not shareholders, but contributors.
shareholders 주주 contributors 기여자/후원자 donations 기부/출연
📌 숫자 발음: 2013 = "twenty-thirteen"
Q2
What does your organization do?
In addition to our investment division, we also operate business divisions focused on startup incubation. Our key programs include a batch program and global mentorship initiatives. We aim to create synergy between these programs and our investments, so our funding decisions are closely aligned with our existing support programs.
investment division 투자 부문 incubation 인큐베이션 batch program 배치 프로그램 synergy 시너지 aligned with ~와 얼라인
Q3
What sectors are you mainly interested in?
For Korea, we invest only in teams selected through our batch program, which are primarily in IT and deep tech. For our global funds, we look for VCs who can effectively support Korean startups in expanding overseas markets.
deep tech 딥테크 global funds 글로벌 펀드 overseas markets 해외 시장
Q4
How is your investment committee process structured?
We accept proposals on a rolling basis. Our IC is held quarterly because we need to invite external members. One key condition for our LP commitments is that the fund must invest a certain amount into Korean startups, which is negotiable.
rolling basis 수시 접수 quarterly 분기별 LP commitments LP 약정 negotiable 협의 가능한 IC = Investment Committee
📌 발음 주의: negotiable = [니고셔블]

Week 4 · Executive Presence

리더십 커뮤니케이션 영상

▶ YouTube 영상 열기
  1. 1
    영상을 0.75 배속, 자막 없이 2회 시청. 어려운 부분을 파악하세요.

    YouGlish에서 어려운 표현을 검색해 쉐도잉 연습하세요.
  3. 3
    15–20초 분량을 선택해 발화자와 유사한 발음/억양으로 녹음해보세요.
📄 스크립트 (00:00 – 00:46)
Every time you speak, people are making a decision.

Is this someone I can trust/ or is this someone that I tune out of?

Running my company, acquisition.com, taught me this.

Leadership lives or dies by your communication.

If you cannot communicate like a leader, then you will never be trusted like one.

So let me show you the five behaviors that are quietly killing your executive presence, and exactly what to do instead.

Number one is stop overexplaining.

You can have the best ideas in the room, but if you can't actually communicate them clearly, you will always follow somebody who can.

And the thing is that trust erodes with every weak behavior that you let slide. The most intelligent people in the room rarely say the most — they usually say the least, but every word lands.
🟡 노란 하이라이트 = 핵심 표현 / 쉐도잉 추천 구간
핵심 표현 정리
executive presence 임원다운 존재감 tune out 무시하다 / 흘려듣다 trust erodes 신뢰가 무너지다 let slide 그냥 넘어가다 overexplaining 과도하게 설명하다
